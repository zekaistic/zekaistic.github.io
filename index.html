Intro

Hi there! I'm Ze Kai, a 21-year-old incoming undergraduate at the National University of Singapore, pursuing a Bachelor of Computing in Computer Science. I am a self-driven individual and an independent learner with a strong interest in programming. I deeply enjoy problem-solving and collaborating with others to create solutions and products that would benefit society as a whole.


I decided to create this simple portfolio while contemplating what to do for my CS50x final project. I could have developed an app or worked on something more beneficial for the community, but in the end, I chose to create this website for myself, as it's something I had always wanted.

Life

Since the tender age of five, I've been playing table tennis. I reached a skill level that allowed me to join the Singapore Junior Development Squad during primary school. Subsequently, I secured admission to Hwa Chong Institution through Direct School Admission via table tennis, which was where I studied from 2016 to 2021.

In 2022, I enlisted in the military for my compulsory two-year National Service, and I ended up as an armament technician for the 9th Army Maintenance Base. As a member of the Forward Maintenance Platoon, our duty was to support the frontlines, primarily the infantry. Life was challenging, and we even participated in Exercise Wallaby, where I created both fond memories and some not-so-good ones.

After becoming operationally ready, I've been enjoying myself by going on overseas trips and spending time with friends! Additionally, I've been trying to find a pre-u internship while undertaking courses such as Harvard's CS50 AI, as a means to refresh and learn new coding knowledge.

Projects


Photography Website

For me, photography is not merely about freezing moments or achieving some sort of aesthetic appeal; it is also a means of conveying emotions, evoking memories, and celebrating happy moments in our lives. Hence, I decided to create a website that showcases my photography.


This project wasn't too difficult to put together - it was relatively easy to reference a template online, comprehend and subsequently modify HTML, CSS and Javascript code. However, a key takeaway from this endeavour was ensuring the optimization of website resources to guarantee a positive user experience - I faced issues such as lagging, incomplete image rendering, and even animation hiccups, prompting me to enhance my skills to overcome these issues.



If you would like to take a look at the website, you can access it here at zekai.pics.
