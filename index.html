Intro

Hi there! I'm Ze Kai, a 21-year-old incoming undergraduate at the National University of Singapore, pursuing a Bachelor of Computing in Computer Science. I am a self-driven individual and an independent learner who has a strong interest in programming. I deeply enjoy problem-solving and working with others to create solutions and products that would benefit society as a whole.


I decided to make this simple portfolio as I was contemplating on what to do for my CS50x final project. I could have made an app, or made something more beneficial for the community, but in the end I decided to make this as I had always wanted a website, simply for myself.


Hobbies

Recently, I got myself a Fujifilm X100V, and I've been taking my time to enjoy the process of learning more about the intricate world of photography. I've been well and truly enjoying myself, and I hardly go anywhere without my camera now, to the dismay of my backpacks.

In addition, I've been making mechanical keyboards since 2021, a hobby that's tedious, even frustrating at times, but fulfilling.

Life

Since the ripe age of five, I've always been playing table tennis. I was good enough to enter the Singapore Junior Development Squad in primary school, and I ended up at Hwa Chong Institution as my secondary school of my choice via Direct School Admission using table tennis, which was where I studied from 2016 - 2021.


In 2022, I enlisted into the military for my compulsory 2-year National Service, where I ultimately ended up as a armament technician for the 9th Army Maintenance Base. As part of the Forward Maintenance Platoon, our job was to support the frontlines of the army, namely infantry. Life was tough, and we even had to partake in Exercise Wallaby, where I made some good memories, and some not-so-good ones.


After I became operationally ready, I've been enjoying myself by going on some overseas trips and enjoying myself with friends! Also, I've been trying to find a pre-u internship while undertaking courses such as Harvard's CS50x, as a means to refresh and learn new coding knowledge.
